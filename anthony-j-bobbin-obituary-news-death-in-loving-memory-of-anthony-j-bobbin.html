Anthony J. Bobbin Obituary News- Death; In Loving Memory of Anthony J. Bobbin

September 2024 Â· 2 minute read

Lehigh Valley Hospital-Cedar Crest in Allentown was the location where Anthony J. Bobbin, who was 75 years old and from Allentown, passed away on December 13, 2023.

As the son of the late Albert Bobbin and Sophie (Gawrylik) Bobbin, he was born in Shenandoah on November 5, 1948. His parents have now passed away.

After graduating from the school that was formerly known as Shenandoah Catholic High School in 1966, Tony continued his education at Stevens Trade School. For a considerable amount of time, he was employed in the steel sector as an electrical maintenance serviceman.

Additionally to his profession, he worked as a handyman in his own home. Tony was a fan of old cars and riding his motorcycle. He also enjoyed riding his motorcycle. He engaged in activities such as skydiving and scuba diving, demonstrating his adventurous nature.

A sister named Ann Joyce Bobbin, who passed away in the year 2020, and a brother named Michael Francis Bobbin are among the people who came before Tony.

Marianne (Kutskiel) Bobbin, his wife of 47 years, his daughter Jennifer Holland and her husband Richard of New York, New York, his two sisters Susan Mary Bobbin of Shenandoah and Mary Swies with her husband Paul of Frackville, and his grandson Ronan are among the members of his family who have survived his passing.

On the 19th of December, there will be a visitation for family and friends at the Oravitz Home for Funerals Inc., which is located at 40 N. Jardin Street in Shenandoah. The visitation will take place from 9 to 11 am. Scripture services are scheduled to take place at eleven in the morning, and Deacon Jack Quirk will preside over them. At a time that is most convenient for the family, the burial will take place.

Donations to the Hillside SPCA in Pottsville, which can be sent to the following address: 51 SPCA Rd, Pottsville, Pennsylvania 17901, are being requested by the family in place of flowers.
