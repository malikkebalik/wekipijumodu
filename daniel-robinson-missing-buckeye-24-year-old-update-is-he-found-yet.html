Daniel Robinson Missing Update: Is he Found yet?

As of September 21, 2023, Daniel Robinson’s whereabouts remain unknown, and the inquiry into his disappearance persists.

Although nearly two years have passed since he was last seen, his family, spearheaded by his father David Robinson II, persists in their relentless search for him.

This case has prompted inquiries into the handling of missing persons cases and underscores the imperative for increased attention to such incidents.

As of the most recent report, the geologist’s location remains unaccounted for.

The circumstances surrounding his vanishing are perplexing, compounded by conflicting pieces of information that have rendered the search exceedingly challenging.

Despite exhaustive endeavors by law enforcement, private investigators, and his family, no conclusive information regarding his location has emerged.

In the latest update dated May 31, 2023, the Buckeye Police Department divulged fresh details about the case.

Geolocation data retrieved from his mobile phone placed it at the crash site approximately at 10:05 am on the morning he went missing.

Moreover, an extensive forensic scrutiny of his electronic devices yielded no substantial leads.

Detectives also briefed the FBI Phoenix Field Office about the case and probed an alleged sighting by an individual who asserted encountering the geologist  near the Hassayampa river bottom on the day of his disappearance.

However, the digital evidence failed to corroborate the information provided by the reporting party.

Despite the passage of time and the formidable challenges encountered during this investigation, the family of Robinson remains resolute in their quest for answers.

The missing man’s father, continues to lead the search efforts, maintaining an unwavering belief that his son is alive and well.

He pledges an unyielding commitment to continue the search until his son is located.

Daniel Robinson Missing Case Details

Daniel Robinson, a 24-year-old geologist, vanished on June 23, 2021, after leaving a job site in Buckeye, Arizona.

He was driving his 2017 blue-grey Jeep Renegade toward the desert and was reported missing later that day.

The geologist was a 5’8″ African-American weighing 165 pounds, with black hair, brown eyes, and a missing right forearm, including his hand.

He had recently graduated as a field geologist in 2019 and had moved to Phoenix for work.

On July 19, 2021, a rancher discovered his overturned Jeep in a ravine.

Airbags were deployed, indicating a crash, and his personal belongings, including his cell phone, wallet, keys, and clothes, were found inside.

In late July, a human skull was found nearby, but it was confirmed not to be the missing 24-year-old, and no further remains were located.

Despite extensive searches covering over 70 square miles and utilizing resources like UTVs, cadaver dogs, drones, and helicopters, no significant leads emerged in the investigation.

Concerns arose when experts hired by Robinson’s family questioned the crash scene’s authenticity.

They noted that the Jeep’s ignition had been repeatedly turned over after the airbags deployed, and extra mileage was recorded post-crash.

Text messages revealed that he had interacted with a woman he met while working for Instacart.

Their encounters grew uncomfortable, with him making unannounced visits to her home.

While initial concerns about his mental state were raised, friends, family, and coworkers denied any signs of suicidal tendencies.

On November 9, 2021, a second set of human remains was found during the search, but preliminary examinations suggested they did not belong to the 24-year-old.

The mystery surrounding the missing man’s disappearance remains unsolved, leaving both his family and investigators searching for answers about his whereabouts.

Daniel Robinson Family Efforts

Robinson’s family has voiced strong criticism regarding the local law enforcement’s efforts to locate their son, leading them to take matters into their own hands.

They established a GoFundMe page and initiated a petition to maintain public interest in the case.

Their proactive approach extended to participating in the Facebook Watch series, Red Table Talk, where they openly discussed the challenges they encountered while collaborating with investigators in the search for their son.

Moreover, the family’s persistence in seeking answers has resonated on social media platforms.

Many individuals on social media have drawn attention to the disparity in national attention between his case and that of Gabby Petito, another missing person.

This contrast has sparked discussions about the disparities in media coverage and the resources allocated to different missing persons cases.

Despite facing obstacles and minimal progress from authorities, the geologist’s family persists in their unwavering pursuit of the truth about their son’s disappearance.

They utilize grassroots efforts and media platforms to keep Robinson’s case in the public eye.
