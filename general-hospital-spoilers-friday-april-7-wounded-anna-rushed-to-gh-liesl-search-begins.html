General Hospital Spoilers: Friday, April 7 Wounded Anna Rushed to GH Liesl Search Begins

September 2024 · 3 minute read

General Hospital (GH) spoilers for Friday, April 7, tease that Bobbie Spencer (Jacklyn Zeman) will introduce the act that everyone’s been waiting for, so it looks like that’ll be her intro for Magic Milo and the Magic Wands.

Cody Bell (Josh Kelly) agreed to be part of the performance, but he’ll have a terrible surprise waiting for him once he’s finished.

Gladys Corbin (Bonnie Burroughs) planted her expensive, borrowed bracelet in Cody’s jacket backstage, so she’s framing him for theft.

Once Sasha Gilmore (Sofia Mattsson) realizes the bracelet’s gone, Gladys may point the finger at a panicked Cody.

Dante Falconeri (Dominic Zamprogna) will have to investigate his buddy, so that’ll lead to a tricky situation.

Someone will be arrested on Friday’s GH episode, but that could be Linc Brown (Dan Buran) since Dante just dragged him off stage following his predator exposure.

However, Cody’s arrest certainly seems to be in the works as well since Diane Miller (Carolyn Hennesy) will accept a new client during the week of April 10-14.

That lines up with Friday’s spoilers about Sonny Corinthos (Maurice Benard) and Sam McCall (Kelly Monaco) offering help.

Sam may decide she believes Cody’s side of the story – and Sonny may offer to hook Cody up with Diane for a lawyer.

It turns out Sonny will have another problem on his mind, so this could tie in with Liesl Obrecht’s (Kathleen Gati) disappearance.

Obrecht vanished during the magic act and never resurfaced, so Maxie Jones (Kirsten Storms) will share some alarming updates with Nina Reeves (Cynthia Watros).

Nina will spill the news to Sonny, who’ll apparently recruit Dex Heller (Evan Hofer) to search for Liesl and do some digging about what might’ve happened to her.

Of course, GH fans already know since one of Victor Cassadine’s (Charles Shaughnessy) goons kidnapped Liesl and put her in a car trunk.

Now that Victor has fired a shot at Anna Devane (Finola Hughes), he’ll flee from the safe house before the police can stop him.

It looks like Victor will end up on The Haunted Star and tell a henchman to make sure they’re prepared for their guests.

Liesl is guest number one, but it seems Victor plans to take Spencer Cassadine (Nicholas Chavez) and Ace Cassadine (Jay and Joey Clay), too.

Spencer will try to talk things out with Trina Robinson (Tabyana Ali) following their latest fight, but he’ll give the wrong answer during their conversation.

Trina will remain frustrated with Spencer’s sense of entitlement and the way he always throws his privilege around.

Unfortunately, the Cassadine name is also a curse since Spencer has menacing people like Victor trying to control him.

Will Spencer get kidnapped as well – or could he perhaps go willingly to protect Ace?

There’s even a chance Victor will leave Spencer behind if he can’t get him to fall in line.

Regardless, Victor will refuse to let Esme Prince (Avery Pohl) raise Ace and should send someone after the baby soon enough.

Esme will seem on edge as she warns Laura Collins (Genie Francis) not to touch her son, so maybe she’ll have a bad feeling.

Mother’s intuition may tell Esme that something terrible is about to happen – and she’ll be right once Victor makes his move!

Next, Lucy Coe (Lynn Herring) will sound the alarm about Victor’s safe house attack. Jordan Ashford (Tanisha Harper) will eventually find out Anna’s been shot, so she’ll fear for her friend’s life.

Robert Scorpio (Tristan Rogers) will get a horrible phone call about Anna’s injuries, so he’ll vow to head to the hospital soon after.

General Hospital spoilers say Anna will find herself in a desperate fight to survive, so stay tuned for all the gripping drama ahead.
