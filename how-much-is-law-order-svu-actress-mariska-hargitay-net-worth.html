Mariska Hargitay Net Worth

Let’s take a look at how Mariska’s pay has evolved throughout the years. Based on data from reports, Mariska Hargitay was paid between $375,000 and $385,000 for each episode between seasons 1 and 10. Those royalties bring in a huge income for her
