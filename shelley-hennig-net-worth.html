Shelley Hennig net worth:

$1 million

Shelley Hennig is an American actress who first became recognized as a beauty pageant title holder since she was crowned Miss Teen USA in 2004. Right after that, she started a career on television when she starred on the soap opera Days of Our Lives and she played her role for several years. After her stint with the soap, she was cast on the TV series The Secret Circle, though it was short-lived. Her next stint on the small screen was as a recurring character on Zach Stone Is Gonna Be Famous. 2014 proved to be more prolific for the former beauty queen as she made her film debut in the horror feature Ouija and became part of the recurring, and later main cast of the MTV series Teen Wolf, earning her further prominence as an actress.
