Biography:

D. J. Cotrona was born on May 23, 1980 in New Haven, Connecticut, U.S. He studied law at the Northeastern University in Boston. He decided to pursue a career in acting instead and went on to move to Los Angeles. In 2003, he made his television debut in an episode of Law & Order: Special Victims Unit.

That same year, he played Adam Roam in the Fox serial Skin. He next made his film debut in Venom, followed by a performance in the movie Love is the Drug. Cotrona then starred as Sean Mathers in the short-lived series Windfall, which aired on NBC. From 2010 to 2011, he starred as John Stone in Detroit 1-8-7. He then played the role of Seth Gecko in From Dusk till Dawn: The Series, which he starred in between 2014 and 2016.
