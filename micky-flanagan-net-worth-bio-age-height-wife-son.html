Who is Micky Flanagan?

Micky Flanagan is an American comedian. He hosted a show on Radio 4 called Micky Flanagan: What Chance Change? He also has been on several TV shows like Mock the Week, A League of Their Own, 8 Out of 10 Cats, Was It Something I Said?, Michael McIntyre’s Comedy Roadshow, and I Love My Country.

He released a stand-up DVD called Back in the Game Live in 2013.

Before he became a comedian, he wanted to be a school teacher. His first job was working as a fish porter at Billingsgate Fish Market. He decided to become a professional comedian in 1997 after taking a comedy course at Jacksons Lane.

In 2007, he was nominated for Best Newcomer at the Edinburgh Comedy Awards for his show What Chance Change?
