Lily Gladstone is an American actress. After deciding to pursue an acting career, she started working in the 2010s. In 2012, she appeared in the French film Jimmy P: Psychotherapy of a Plains Indian. The following year, she got a part in the Hollywood movie, Winter in the Blood. A few years later, Gladstone garnered wider recognition for her role in the drama film Certain Women. Playing the role of The Rancher, her performance was acclaimed, getting nominations like the Independent Spirit Award. Following this, she starred in the films Buster’s Mal Heart, Walking Out, and First Cow.
