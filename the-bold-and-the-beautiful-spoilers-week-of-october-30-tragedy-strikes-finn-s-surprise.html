The Bold and the Beautiful (B&B) spoilers for the week of October 30-November 3 reveal that Eric Forrester (John McCook) and Ridge Forrester’s (Thorsten Kaye) fashion showdown will reach an exciting climax.

The buyers will choose which anonymous designs they like best, so Ridge and Eric will know that they’re getting a fair result based on their work alone.

Of course, someone presumably still has to lose this couture competition. Ridge has been gloating nonstop about how he’s going to crush Eric on the runway, but he wouldn’t be doing that if he knew his father was fading fast.

If Ridge comes out on top, he’s going to regret stealing what could be Eric’s last moment of glory on that stage.

On the flip side, Eric could pull off a victory instead, but it may cost him since Dr. Colin Colby (Justiin Davis) warned that moving ahead with this competition could be fatal!

A tie might be possible if B&B wants to highlight that both Ridge and Eric are talented in their own ways, but the real drama will come after the showdown regardless.

Although Eric was given six months to live, it could be less based on the details Donna Logan (Jennifer Gareis) offered from Dr. Colby.

That’s especially true since Eric isn’t listening to Dr. Colby’s advice about taking it easy.

Eric will seemingly just keep going until he physically can’t anymore – and once that happens, he just might keel over!

The Bold and the Beautiful spoilers say Brooke will realize something that shocks her in the week ahead.

That could involve Eric’s looming grim fate if Brooke learns about his diagnosis from one of her sisters or perhaps finds Eric unconscious.

Whatever the case, the writing’s on the wall for Eric to collapse, so he may get rushed to the hospital once the fashion faceoff concludes.

Next, Steffy Forrester (Jacqueline MacInnes Wood) will return to Los Angeles and stun John “Finn” Finnegan (Tanner Novlan) when she reunites with him at home.

Liam Spencer (Scott Clifton) may be disappointed to learn Steffy came back to Finn instead of him, but he won’t give up on his goal of reuniting with Steffy one day.

In the meantime, Steffy will want to talk to Finn about Sheila Carter’s (Kimberlin Brown) relationship with Deacon Sharpe (Sean Kanan) and why he delayed delivering that news.

The Bold and the Beautiful spoilers say Finn will hope to explain and get his marriage back on track, so stay tuned for updates on his desperate efforts.
