JD Pardo Birthday, Parents & Early Life

The 5 feet 11 inches tall Jorge Daniel Pardo, aka JD Pardo, was born on September 7, 1980, to his Salvadoran mother and Argentinean father in the Panorama City Neighborhood of Los Angeles, California.
