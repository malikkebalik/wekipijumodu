Each Cast Member's 10 Survival Gear Items

August 2024 · 3 minute read

What does it take to survive in the wild? On Alone, survivalists must rely on their wits, along with 10 carefully chosen tools, if they hope to outlast their competitors and win the show’s $500,000 grand prize. 

Each contestant is dropped at their campsite with a mix of items chosen from a pre-approved list of survival gear. Every cast member is also allowed to bring a number of clothing and personal care items that don’t count toward their personal limit. They also have a first aid kit and, of course, camera gear so they can film their journey. 

So, what tools did the Alone Season 11 cast decide to bring with them on their journey to the Arctic Circle? Some items were universal. Unsurprisingly, everyone selected a sleeping bag and a pot for cooking. Others were widely rejected — no contestant chose food rations. But there were also a few surprising choices.

Peter Albano

Peter Albano, 42, is a librarian from British Columbia. His 10 items are: 

  • Sleeping Bag
  • 2-Quart Pot
  • Ferro Rod
  • Bow and Arrows
  • Fishing Line and Hooks
  • Snare Wire
  • Multitool
  • Saw
  • Paracord
  • Shovel
